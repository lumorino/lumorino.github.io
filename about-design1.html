Education Bridge

Aged 21, I founded Education Bridge, aka YuQiao (语桥), in Tianjin, China. I spent more than five years of my life on this amazing project. Education Bridge was an ESL (English as a Second Language) EdTech company that provided services to private kindergartens and primary schools. Our main innovations were our business plan and our teaching IP. Although we worked with a 3 to 12 year old range, through time we became highly specialised in the 3 to 7 range.
